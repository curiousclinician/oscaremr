9         ADDING STAFF TO A PROGRAM/ ADDING QUATROSHELTER ROLE IN QUATROSHELTER

 

a)  Click on the grey “Program” tab

 

Click “Program” Tab
 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

b) Click on “Staff List”

 

Click “Staff List”
 

c) Click on “Edit” beside the staff member who you would like to add to a program.

 

Click “Edit” beside staff name
 

d) Click the grey tab entitled “Programs”

 

Click the “Programs” tab
 

 

 

 

 

 

e) Under the heading of Role click on the drop down menu and click the staff member’s role. 

Click on the staff member’s role.
 

 

f) Once you click the role a green check mark will appear to show that the staff member has now been added to the program.

 

A green check mark appears when staff member is added to a program
 

 

 

*Repeat steps 1-4 for each staff member you are adding to a program*
