2.12.7  Family Intake

 

In a family shelter scenario users will need to process a family intake. The family intake process starts by processing an individual intake for the head of the family. Then a family intake would be processed for the rest of the family members.

 

The family intake process can be accessed by pressing the Family Intake tab on the Intake Form.

Family Intake Form.

The family Intake Form is used to maintain the information about the family members of the family belonging to the head of a family. From the Family Intake Form the user can maintain the information for the spouse and dependants.
