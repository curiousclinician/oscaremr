6         Administration

 

6.1           Assigning access to QUATROSHELTER

 

Click on the “Admin” tab at the top of the Opening page.

This will take you to the “Administrative page”

 

 

 

6.2           Add a provider  

 

1)       Adding a new staff (user) to QUATROSHELTER is a simple process.  You must be an administrator to add new users. 

a)     Under Provider click on “Search/Edit/ Delete Provider Records” to see if the staff member already has a provider record.

 

Click “Search/Edit/Delete Provider Records”
 

                    

b) Enter the front line workers last name into the search engine and click “Search”. -If they do have a provider record and have forgotten their password then skip to section 2.0 of this user guide entitled: “What to do when providers forget their password and/or Pin Number”.  If not then do the following:

 

-Click the “Back” space button.

 

 

Click “Back”
 

Enter last name and click “Search”
 

 

STEP ONE

 

c)  Under Provider click “Search/Edit/Delete Provider Records”

 

Click “Search/Edit/ Delete Provider Records”
 


 

d)  Without entering anything into the search box click “Search”

 

Click “Search”
 

e)  Find an unused Provider No [ this is done by searching a list of existing providers, organize them in ascending order by Provider No, find a number that is not in use]

-*****DO NOT START ANY PROVIDER NUMBER WITH A ZERO (i.e. 009, 0003)*****

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

f) You can click “ID” to put numbers in chronological order.

Once you have a number click the “Back” button.

 

Click “Back”

 

Click “ID”

 

 

STEP TWO

a) Under Provider click “Add a Provider Record”

 

Click “Add a Provider Record”

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

b) Type in the staff’s provider number, last name and first name

-Choose the type of provider (most staff will fall under the “nurse” category)

-Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on “Add Provider Record”

-Next you must assign a role to provider.

 

When done filling in click “Add Provider Record”
 

Type in: Provider No, Last Name, First name and Type.
 

 

STEP THREE

 

6.3           Assigning a role and a login to the provider

In OSCAR: role assignment

a) On the Admin page under Security click “Assign Role to Provider”

 

Click “Assign role to Provider”

 

 

b) Find the provider on the list, you can press the CTRL key and F at the same time to bring up a search engine to search the last name of the provider.

c) Beside the provider’s name select the role you wish to add (i.e. add doctor role if you are a doctor or nurse, nurse role if counsellor, etc, the most common role to add for most staff is “nurse”)

-Click “Add” beside the staff member’s  name

-More then one role can be added if needed

-Once the provider is added successfully. Close this box

- Next you must add a login record for the provider

 

Once done close this box by clicking on the red X
 

Select Role, and Click “Add”
 

STEP FOUR

6.4           Add a login record for the provider

 

a) First check if the person already has a temporary login through the search login function, if they do click on that record and once in screen choose to delete the record. This will delete their password.  To do this see section 2.0 “What to do when providers forget their Password and/or PIN number”

 

b) From the Admin Page under Security click “Add a Login Record”

 

Click “Add a Login Record”
 

 

 

 

 

 

c) Let the provider type in the following fields:

-“User Name”

-“Password” and “Confirm”

-“PIN” number and “Confirm”

 

-Click on the drop down box and choose the providers name in the “Provider  

   No.” field

-Check expiry date and choose a date a few years from now – it is important that every person has an expiry date for technical reasons

-The agency needs to decide who has remote access, if provider needs remote access then check “remote access” box by PIN, if not then make sure the box is not selected

-Select box beside “local access”

-Have the provider select a PIN number that they will remember (i.e. four digits in phone number

-Click “Add Record”

 

***Log out of the system and have the new provider try their new login***

 

As the administrator fill in “Provider No, Expiry Date, and Pin (local) Enable”.  Then click “Add Record”
 

Have the provider fill in:

“User Name, Password, Confirm, Pin and Confirm”.
