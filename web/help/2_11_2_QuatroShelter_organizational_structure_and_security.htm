2.11.2  QuatroShelter organizational structure and security levels

 

The base components of QuatroShelter’s Organizational structure (ORG) are the System, Organizations, Shelters, Facilities and Programs. Security can be issued at any of these levels. In QuatroShelter the ORG is maintained automatically as the codes and relationships of Organizations, Shelters, Facilities and Programs are created and maintained by the system administrator.
