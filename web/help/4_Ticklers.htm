4         Ticklers

 

-  The Tickler+ screen is used to alert assigned staff to an action that needs to be addressed via a message

 

4.1           Adding a Tickler

 

-  Ticklers are added by selecting “Add a Tickler” in the client’s CME

-   In the search engine enter the Last Name of the provider you wish to assign the              tickler to and press “Search”

-   Write message and Select “Priority Level” (i.e. normal, urgent). 

-   Click “Save” once you are done writing your tickler– the tickler will become active immediately; however, the red indicator (!) beside a person’s name may not show up right away.

 

4.2           Viewing a Tickler

 

-  Ticklers can be viewed from the CME or from the Tickler tab

-  In the CME: click on “View a Tickler”

-  In the Tickler tab: ticklers are viewed by setting the filters at the top (status, provider, client, task assigned to)

-  Providers can also filter out their own ticklers by selecting “My Ticklers” from the    custom tickler drop down in the Tickler tab

 

4.3           Using a custom filter to view a Tickler

 

-  Providers can also use the “CUSTOM FILTERS” to make their own custom filters that      will filter out ticklers as they would like (ex. Administrators can create a new provider called PROGRAM X STAFF – staff can assign ticklers for everyone to this provider. Staff can then make a custom filter to receive all ticklers sent to PROGRAM X STAFF)

-  Providers can change their preferences so that a small pop-up window will appear that tells them how many ticklers are assigned to them. Go into “Preferences” tab and select “Enable” beside “New Tickler Warning”.

-  When the assigned staff have read and completed the task they check the box on   the left and scroll to the bottom of the page and click complete. All ticklers are stored by status (i.e. active, complete, and deleted).

 

-  By clicking on the MAGINIFYING GLASS icon on the left of the tickler, you will see   the expanded view of the tickler. You can print it and, or add a message to accompany the original tickler. (i.e.: could not complete this task because client was not found)
