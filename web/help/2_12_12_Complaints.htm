2.12.12                    Complaints

 

Complaints from clients can be posted by the stuff by accessing the Complaints function. A complaints form is used to process the complaint.

 

Complaints list.

The complaints list shows all the complaints posted for a client. The complaints can be seen in detail by clicking on the Compliant ID. The status of the complaint is also shown on the list. The status can be active or completed.
