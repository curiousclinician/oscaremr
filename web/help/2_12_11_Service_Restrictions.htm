2.12.11                    Service Restrictions

 

Service restrictions may be placed on specific clients. The service restrictions functions provide the user the tools to create and manage the service restrictions for a client. A service restriction may stop the client from being admitted to a program where the restriction was placed.

 

Service restriction list.       

 

The service restriction lists all the service restrictions posted for a client. The status of each service restriction is shown. The status of a service restriction is active or completed. There are two ways that a service restriction can be completed: the first is that the time established for the service restriction expires. The second is by manually terminating the restriction by press Terminate Early button on the discharge list.

 

 

 

 

Service restriction form.

 

The service restriction form is used to post a service restriction on a client.
