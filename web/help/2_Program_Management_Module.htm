2          Program Management Module

 

2.1            Accessing Program Management

 

Clicking on the grey “program tab” at the top of the screen takes you into the QUATROSHELTER Program Management   

 

 

 

There are two primary screens to the QUATROSHELTER program to get familiar with. First is the

OSCAR medical screen which allows you to get into the CASE MANAGEMENT ENCOUNTER (CME) and allows you to do individual case management. 

Second is the program tab which allows you to get into the QUATROSHELTER screen which is the PROGRAM MANAGEMENT MODULE (PMM) which allows you to do program management.  The primary functions of each screen are as follows:

 

2.2            Viewing client files

 

2.2.1      In OSCAR Medical:

-  When you sign in, the opening screen shows all client files in the selected program.

-  Beside client names you will see the icons that your role needs to have access to:

E = Encounters (where all of the case management functions are accessed)

QUATROSHELTER is set up to allow role-based access within programs. This means that if you are identified as a counselor then you will not have access to encounters left by doctors that pertain to medical information (unless you are a part of that specific program or team). 

 

2.2.2      In QUATROSHELTER:

 

- To view a client in QUATROSHELTER, click on the “Clients Name” while in OSCAR

OR

- To get to QUATROSHELTER, the Program Management Module (PMM), click on the“program tab” along the top row of tabs in OSCAR

 

- Click “search for client” on the navigator bar on the left. Enter some of the client’s     information in the search boxes

- Selecting “search outside of domain” means you will search for client who is not in one of the programs you have access to, “Soundex” means that the system will look for similar last names – taking into account spelling errors

-Select the client you are looking for by clicking on their name

-You will then be looking at the summary page which has personal information, Intake Form information, consent form, Current Programs and Referrals. You can click on any of the tabs across the top for alternate information. The tabs are as follows:

“History”: the history tab documents all the program movement and referrals

“Forms”: This tab holds the intake B and any user created forms

“Refer”: This tab is where you will do referrals

“Discharge”: This is where you will complete discharges to the community and discharge clients from service and temporary programs

 

2.3            Searching for a Client

 

2.3.1      In OSCAR Medical:

 

If you’re unable to find a clients file, you can search the system for the file by entering the last name of the client in the search box at the top of the page

-  If you cannot find the client, do a client search in QUATROSHELTER

 

2.3.2      In QUATROSHELTER:

 

-  You can search the system for the file by clicking on the “Client Search” tab on the Navigator bar to the left of the page.

-  Using the filters at the top of the search page you can search by name for the file. -SEARCH OUTSIDE OF DOMAIN allows you to look for clients who are outside of your programs. SOUNDEX ON NAMES allows you to search for names that sound like the one you have entered (incase of spelling errors). These are selected by default.

-  If you still cannot find the client’s file you must create a new one by filling out an Intake form, located on the navigation bar click “New Client”

 

2.4      Adding a new client

 

- New clients are created by completing an intake.

- Go to the PMM and click “New Client” on the navigator bar on the left hand side.

- When “New Client” is selected, a search engine will pop up. You must enter in some identifying data and search for the client before you begin a new intake. This is to eliminate the occurrence of duplicate files. A list will be generated of similar last names, in order to see if the one on the list matches your client. If there is a match to your client, you can click on the icon beside their name and update the intake. After you save this, it will open to the PMM summary page. At this point you can make any relevant referrals

 If there are no names in the database that match the client then the Intake form will directly open

- In the intake A sheet fill in:

                                                - First and last name.

                                                - Date of birth.

                                                - Health insurance number and version code.

- EFF Date is the expiry date of the health card

- Any other pertinent information

 

- Before you save the intake, you must admit them to a program otherwise they will be placed in a HOLDING TANK by default.

- Select a program from the list generated by the drop down menu – (only bed programs that are not full to capacity will be visible)

- If you have access to a bed program and it is not visible in your admission program list, that program is most likely full. Revisit the program information page and double check the program capacity.

- When you have filled in as much information as is available, click the “add record” or “Save” button at the bottom of the screen.

- You can refer a client to additional programs when completing the intake form or by selecting the “refer” tab on the top of the QUATROSHELTER client page.  Please see “Referring a Client to a Program” as explained below.

 

 

2.5      Bed Management

 

Now reserving and managing beds can be done more efficiently with QUATROSHELTER’s bed management system.

 

Bed Management (Reservation):

-          Click on the client’s name whom you would like to reserve a bed for.   

-          Click the grey tab entitled “Bed Reservation”

-          Assign” bed number” ( bed numbers go in sequential order of last bed given)

-          Assign the “Status”

-          Select the “Date” which the bed will reserved until.

-          Click “Save”

Bed Management (Bed Check):

-          Click on the grey “program” tab.   

-          Click on the appropriate program

-          Click on the grey “bed check” tab at the top of the page

-          Here you will see a list of clients and their bed reservation numbers. You can change the status, extend the stay and discharge a client from the bed program under this function. 

-          Here you can also see a list of the day’s expired bed reservations.

-          At the bottom of the page you can click “Bed Check Report” and print off a list of the current bed reservations

 

2.6      Referring and Admissions

 

- To refer a client to a program make sure that you have searched and selected the appropriate client first

 

- Click on the “Refer Tab.”

 

- If you know the name of the program you want to refer to, enter it in its entirety or partially in the box and click “Search” you can also select the type of program you are looking for either “Bed” or “Service”

 

- If you do not know the name of the program you can leave the box empty and just press search.

 

- This will bring up the pages of the programs available in a separate screen

 

- Click on the program you want to refer to.

 

- Make any notes in the “referral notes” sections if desired, and then click “Process Referral”

 

2.7      Admissions

 

-  To admit a client to a program you must have access to that program.

 

-  In the Program Management Module go the Navigator Bar on the left hand side and click on “Home”

 

-  Select the program that you want to accept a client in.

 

-  The program will open to the Queue.

 

-  Click “Admit “beside the client’s name.

 

-  Write any admission notes, and then click “Process Admission”

 

2.8      Temporary Admissions

 

-  When completing a referral, before you select “Process Referral” there is an option to select “Temporary Admission”. When you select this upon referral, it will allow for the client to be admitted to two bed programs at once – the original bed program and a secondary temporary bed program. The secondary temporary bed program is viewed as a service program in QUATROSHELTER

 

-  To discharge clients from this program please see the discharge section in this manual

 

2.9      Discharging a Client

 

- To discharge a client from a service program, go to their client file in the Program Management Module (by searching for them in search engine or by clicking on their name in the OSCAR Medical screen)

- Click on the “Discharge” tab

-  Select Discharge from a service program, make any notes, and then click “Process Discharge”

-  To discharge from a temporary bed program, you will have to follow the same steps as above but you will be completing them under the “discharge from a temporary program” tab within the discharge tab

 

- To discharge from a QUATROSHELTER program to the community (i.e. the client has left the agency to the community).

 

-  Follow this step when you are discharging the client from your program to the community (i.e. whereabouts unknown, living with partner, left the city, etc.)

 

-  Follow the same steps as above but you will be completing the discharge under “Discharge to a non-QuatroShelter community bed program”

 

-  Once you have discharged a client to a community program – you can access their file in the archive view of the program you discharged them from

 

***To see a detailed description of this process please see the community discharge manual ***

 

2.10Batch Discharge and Admission

 

- To do a batch discharge in the PMM click on the program you would like to move the clients from.

 

-  Click on the grey tab at the top titled “Clients”

 

-  Click in all the boxes beside the clients you would like to batch discharge notice a green arrow will appear.

 

-  From the drop down menu click on the program you would like to admit the clients to.

 

- Then depending on which program you would like to admit the client to you can either

click on the “ batch discharge to a QUATROSHELTER program” or “batch discharge to a community bed program.” 

 

- A window will pop up letting you know that you are discharging clients and letting you

  know where you are discharging them to.

 

- Click “ok”

 

- The clients have now all been admitted to the program you have selected.

 

 

2.11Uploading Pictures of a Client

 

The idea of having client pictures in the case management files came out of client focus groups. The opinion was that picture identification in the case management screen would help prevent identity theft and act as valid identification for that client.

-  Client’s picture will need to be uploaded to computer (from digital camera, scanned in as a document, etc.)

-  In the Case Management Encounter Screen click on the “orange shadow box”.

-  Click on “browse”

-  Find the file and the picture of the client you wish to upload.

-  Click on “upload”

-  You will now notice the shadow box has turned “green”

By hovering the cursor over the green shadow box the client’s picture will appear.  It will stay on the screen for five seconds.  This ensures that the client’s picture is not in plain view for long periods of time.

-  The photo can be removed or updated at any time by following the same steps above
