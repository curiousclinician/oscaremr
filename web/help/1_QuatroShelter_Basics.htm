1           QuatroShelter Basics

This chapter provides instructions on commonly used functions that are performed throughout the entire QuatroShelter application.

 

1.1           Accessing QUATROSHELTER

 

In the address bar type the name of your organization/hospital along with QuatroShelter.ca.

For example: “seaton.QuatroShelter.ca” (no www.)

Answer YES and OKAY to the boxes that appear.

 

 

To Login, enter the following:

·         “user name”

·         “password”

·         “2nd level password” = pin number

 

Click “Sign in” button.

 

 

The opening screen shows the OSCAR medical program.  Clients are listed here according to the program name. This will show the client list for the program that is selected in the drop down menu in the top right hand corner, underneath the Program Tab

 

1.2           Logging Out

 

It is very important that you log out each time you use the system.  If you are in QUATROSHELTER, click OSCAR MEDICAL at the bottom of the navigation bar.  Once in OSCAR, click LOG OUT on the right-hand side.
