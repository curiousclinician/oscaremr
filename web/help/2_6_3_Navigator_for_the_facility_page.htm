2.6.3     Navigator for the facility page.

 

When in the facility page, the Navigator lists the various pages (screens) associated with an individual facility including the general, Bed, Program and Message pages. Some pages or links may not be accessible to all users, the access to these pages and links may be controlled by the system access security. See chart for further information.

By selecting this option users will access the facility’s room and bed creation and management page. The facility’s room and bed information page provide the tools necessary to create and maintain the rooms and beds in a facility and for the facility’s programs.

D

Program

By selecting this option users will access a list of the program’s associated with the facility. The users can then further access the detail information programs.

E

Message

By selecting this option users will access the facility message pages. The facility messages pages provide the users the tools necessary to access and maintain messages that would be shown of the home page of the designated facility
