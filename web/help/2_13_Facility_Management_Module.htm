2.13     Facility Management Module

 

Facilities are a subset of shelters. In general facilities will be defined as a building or major areas where client programs and services are performed for the clients of the shelter. Room and beds are defined within facilities and are assigned to bed program in those facilities.

 

The facility functions work with the Navigator. There are 4 main options available on the Navigator: the General function to create and maintain the basic definition of a facility; the Bed function to access the function to maintain the Rooms and Beds in a facility and assigned those to a program; the Program function that shows a list of Programs that have been assigned to a facility; The Facility message function that allows the broadcasting of messages for specific messages.
