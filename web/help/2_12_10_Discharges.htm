2.12.10                    Discharges

 

Every admission will be completed by a discharge. There are a few ways in which a discharge of a client may happen. A user may manually discharge a client. An automatic discharge may be processed if a client is admitted at another shelter.

 

Discharge list.

 

The discharge list shows all admissions and also shows the status of the discharges. For active admissions an action button “Discharge” would be available. If the discharge button was pressed the user could process a discharge of the client using the discharge form.

 

 

 

Discharge form.

 

The discharge form is used to process a discharge. Once the discharge is processed and saved. The client will be discharged from the program, the admission and intake statuses will be change to ‘discharged’.
