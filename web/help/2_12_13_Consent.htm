2.12.13                    Consent

 

Consent forms from the client for access to information from other clients can be processed using the Complaints function in QuatroShelter. The Consent form needs to be filled and processed. The client is required to sign the consent using the signature pad.

 

Consent form list.

 

The consent list shows all the consent forms processed for the client. The status of the consent forms show on the list. The status can be active or completed. The status of the consent form would change from active to complete by either just expiring or it can also be by revoking the consent.

 

 

 

Consent form.

The consent form needs to be filled by user and the client needs to sign the consent form using the signature pad.
