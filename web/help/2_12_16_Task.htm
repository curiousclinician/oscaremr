2.12.16                    Task

 

Tasks are messages that can be entered by the staff in regards to clients. The tasks are dated and can be sent to a staff in a program to remind them of something, or request of something for them to do in regards to the client.

 

Task list

 

Shows all tasks issued for a client and their status.

 

 

 

 

Task detailed view.

 

Shows all the detailed comment of the task.
