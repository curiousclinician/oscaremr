1.2           Modules

 

QuatroShelter is divided into five main modules: Client Management, Facility Management, Program Management, Reports and Administration.

 

 

MODULES Summary Table

Module

Description

Icon

Client-Management

The client management module provides the tools necessary to manage client’s information. The functions in this module include the searching of clients in the system as well as the addition and maintenance of  information and transactions regarding the client.

 

 

Program Management

The program management module provides the tools necessary to create and maintain the service or bed programs offered by shelters in their facilities. The programs in QuatroShelter are an important part of the application since it is at this level that services available to clients are defined. Areas that are used or maintained in programs are the Queue of the program, a view of the clients currently in the program, incidents reported about a program may be reported and Service Restrictions may be defined for programs.

 

 

Facility Management

The facility management module provides the tools necessary to create and maintain facilities as well as the maintenance of the rooms and beds that are in each facility and its associated programs. Facility messages can also be managed through the facility management module.

 

 

 

Reports

Create reports on PDF format or export your data to Excel files. You can save your report criteria by using the “Save Template” feature and reuse them in the future as required.

 

 

 

Administration

The administration module provides tools necessary to create, maintain and configure the root areas of the system that are necessary for secured access to data and functions. User, roles, functions, role-functions relationships, user-ORG relationships and all lookup tables are managed through the administration module. A few other secured functions are also maintained using the administration module.
