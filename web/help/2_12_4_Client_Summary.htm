2.12.4  Client Summary

 

The client’s summary page displays a selection of the client’s information in QuatroShelter. The information on the summary page includes: Basic Information, the client’s family members if the client is a head of family, the room and bed number assigned to the client if the client is an active client, the list of programs that the client’s is currently active in (Bed and Service programs) and the list of active referrals.
