2.12.15                    Attachment

 

Documents that are in a workstation or in some computer format can be attached to the client record. The documents can be searched in the workstation and once found the document file may be saved on QuatroShelters database.

 

The attachment list.

Shows all the client’s attachments stored in the system.

 

 

New attachments are searched and selected from the user’s workstation, groups in a logical document group such as Education, Personal ID, etc. and saved into the QuatroShelter database.
